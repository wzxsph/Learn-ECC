# Vvodnyye uprazhneniya

Vypolnite sleduyushchiye uprazhneniya dlya zakrepleniya osnov ECC.

## Uprazhneniye 1: Proverka sredy

**Cel'**: Podtverdit', chto sreda ECC ustanovlena pravil'no

**Shagi**:
1. Zapustite testovyy nabor: `node tests/run-all.js`
2. Proverte, chto vse testy proshli
3. Prosmotrite strukturu direktori `.claude/`

**Kriterii priyomki**:
- Vse testy proshli
- Mozhete uvidet' direktoriif agents, skills, hooks, rules

---

## Uprazhneniye 2: Ispol'zovaniye slay-komand

**Cel'**: Oznakomit'sya s chasto ispol'zuyemymi slay-komandami

**Shagi**:
1. Zapustite Claude Code
2. Posledovatel'no vypolnite sleduyushchiye komandy, nablyudayte za vyvodom:
   - `/help` ili `/ecc:plan`
   - `/ecc:code-review`
3. Zapeshchayte format vyvoda kazhdoy komandy

**Kriterii priyomki**:
- Ponyali funktsii kazhdoy komandy i format vyvoda

---

## Uprazhneniye 3: Sozdaniye prostogo Agenta

**Cel'**: Ponyat' format fayla Agenta

**Shagi**:
1. Prosmotrite sushchestvuyushchiye fayly Agent v direktori `agents/`
2. Ispol'zuyte format `planner.md` v kachestve referentsii
3. Sozdadeey prostoy `hello-agent.md`, kotoryy vyvodit privetstviye

**Kriterii priyomki**:
- Novyy format fayla Agenta pravil'nyy
- Soderzhit YAML frontmatter (name, description, tools, model)

---

## Uprazhneniye 4: Zapusk Hook

**Cel'**: Ponyat' mekhanizm Hook

**Shagi**:
1. Prosmotrite konfiguratsii Hook v direktori `hooks/`
2. Zapustite `node scripts/hooks/run-with-flags.js --help` (yesli podderzhivayetsya)
3. Ponyat' vremya srabatyvaniya Hook i logiku ispolneniya

**Kriterii priyomki**:
- Mozhete opisat' printsip raboty Hook
- Ponyali raznitsu mezhdu PreToolUse i PostToolUse

---

## Uprazhneniye 5: Kompleksnoye zadaniye

**Cel'**: Vypolnit' malen'kiy proyekt

**Zadaniye**: Sozdat' instrument formatirovaniya imyon lyudey

**Funktsional'nyye trebovaniya**:
- `formatName(name)` - Formatirovaniye v "Familiya, Imya"
- `initials(name)` - Vozvrashchayet abbriviatury

**Protsess vypolneniya**:
1. Ispol'zuyte `/ecc:plan` dlya planirovaniya
2. Ispol'zuyte `/tdd` dlya razrabotki
3. Ispol'zuyte `/ecc:code-review` dlya proverki

**Kriterii priyomki**:
- Polnost'yu realizovany dve funktsii
- Soderzhit testovyye sluchai
- Proshlo proverku koda
